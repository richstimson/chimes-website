Chimes Product Roadmap

Our vision: To be the leading platform connecting food lovers to amazing experiences through real-time locations and offers – from ice cream vans and food trucks to local restaurants and cafés.

This roadmap outlines Chimes' strategic development across four main areas: Mobile Ice Cream Vendors, Food Trucks & Pop-up Vendors, Restaurants & Cafés, and Advanced Platform Enhancements. Each area contains detailed sub-phases that build upon our core vision of connecting food lovers with amazing culinary experiences.

While presented sequentially, these phases aren't necessarily chronological. Implementation order will be commercially driven based on market research, user feedback, and business opportunities to ensure maximum impact and sustainable growth.

Phase 1: Mobile Ice Cream Vendors

Establishing our foundation with mobile ice cream vendors, focusing on real-time tracking, notifications, and core platform functionality.

🍦Phase 1.1: Core MVP (Mobile Ice Cream Vendors)
Goal: Enable users to find and get notified about nearby mobile ice cream vendors.
✓ Live tracking: Map-based real-time location of ice cream vans.
✓ Push notifications: Notify users when a van is nearby or starting a route.
✓ Customer heatmap (vendor view): Vendors see registered customers' approximate locations (by postcode/area) to help plan stops.
✓ Ice cream chime notification tone
✓ Vendor sign in
✓ Vendor Privacy - vendors control when they are visible to their customers
✓ Help, Share & Follow us screens
✓ Chimesapp website and social media pages
✓ iOS and Android apps released to app stores
✓ Live commercial trial
🚚Phase 1.2: Enhanced User Experience
Goal: Enhanced vendor customisation, advanced privacy controls, location-based features and promotional capabilities.
  • Customised vendor icons
  • Customised vendor notification tones
  • Vendor option to have their own Chimes powered app
  • Enhanced Vendor Privacy - home geofence, rounds geofence and scheduled privacy
  • Mobile Vendor Profiles
  • Mobile Vendor routes & schedules
  • Use customer's current location to create notification zone
  • Notifications when vendors attend public events (e.g. fairs, markets, festivals)
  • Promotions & flash deals: Push notifications for limited-time offers.
  • Simple browse, search & filter
🤖Phase 1.3: AI & Predictive Features (Mobile Vendors)
Goal: Leverage AI to optimise mobile vendor operations and customer engagement.
  • Predictive route planning: Suggest optimal stops based on customer heatmaps, weather, time of day, and local events.
  • Sales analytics: Track most popular locations, peak times, menu popularity.

Phase 2: Food Trucks & Pop-up Vendors

Expanding to the broader food truck ecosystem with comprehensive vendor profiles, AI-powered insights, and predictive capabilities.

🏪Phase 2.1: Expansion to Food Trucks
Goal: Add food trucks and pop-up vendors with customized profiles and customer engagement features.
  • Customised profile added by vendors – description, hours, location, cuisine type
  • Customer ratings for vendors (vendor approved)
  • Push notifications for flash special offers
  • Segmented notifications based on cuisine preference, distance, or behavior
  • Customised Chimes notification tone for vendors
  • Unique discount codes or points collection for proof of using the app
  • Customers selects type of food – ice cream, taco's, burgers, etc when browsing
  • Integration with food outlet databases to add basic profiles (name, location) for all food trucks
  • Promotions & flash deals: Push notifications for limited-time offers
  • Simple browse, search & filter
🤖Phase 2.2: AI & Predictive Features (General)
Goal: Leverage AI to optimise the wider food ecosystem and customer experience.
  • Customer preference forecasting: Predict popular menu items and promotions per location.
  • Dynamic notifications: Automatically target customers likely to engage with certain offers.
  • Trend analysis: Identify emerging food trends in different areas.
  • Vendor recommendations: Suggest new menus or promotions based on predictive insights.
  • AI-driven personalised recommendations.

Phase 3: Restaurants & Cafés

Establishing Chimes as a comprehensive food discovery platform by integrating static food outlets and building a complete ecosystem.

🏪Phase 3.1: Roll-out to Restaurants & Cafes
Goal: Integrate traditional food outlets to become a comprehensive food discovery platform.
  • Vendor profiles with customized descriptions, hours, location, and cuisine types
  • Customer ratings and reviews for static outlets
  • Push notifications for special offers and promotions
  • Location-based discovery and filtering
  • Integration with existing restaurant databases
🌐Phase 3.2: Broader Food Ecosystem
Goal: Become a full-scale local food discovery & engagement platform.
  • Subscription/loyalty tiers for premium users
  • Event & festival integration: Pop-up events, food fairs, night markets
  • Collaborations: Optional delivery partnerships
  • Community features: Reviews, comments, sharing favorite vendors, user-created events

Phase 4: Advanced Platform Enhancements

Advanced features and monetization tools to enhance user engagement and create additional revenue streams across the entire platform.

💼Phase 4.1: Monetization & Business Features
Goal: Implement sustainable revenue streams and advanced business tools for vendors.
  • Premium vendor subscriptions for analytics & promotion tools
  • Sponsored listings & featured vendor placements
  • Loyalty partnerships with brands and vendors
  • In-app marketplace for orders, pre-orders, and meal bundles
  • Advanced interactive features and community monetization
🚀Phase 4.2: Advanced Platform Interactivity
Goal: Enhance user engagement and platform interactivity through advanced features and interactive tools.
  • In-app chat – simple customer ⇄ vendor messaging
  • In-app order & payment integration for pre-orders or queue management
  • Interactive notification zones: Create zones by pointing and clicking on the map
  • Custom notification zone creation (not just a circle)
  • Reservation/pre-order notifications
