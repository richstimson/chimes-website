## What is Chimes?

Chimes is a revolutionary app that helps ice cream lovers find their favourite vans in real-time. When customers are craving ice cream, they open Chimes to see which vans are operating nearby and get notifications when their favourites are in the area.

## How Chimes Benefits Your Business

🎯 **Get Discovered** - Customers actively search for ice cream vans using Chimes, putting you directly in front of people who want to buy

📍 **Location-Based Marketing** - Your van appears on the map when you're operating, attracting nearby customers who might not have known you were there

🔔 **Build a Following** - Customers can follow your van to get notifications when you're in their area, creating a loyal customer base

📈 **Increase Sales** - More visibility means more customers finding you throughout the day

💝 **Customer Loyalty** - Regular customers never miss you again, leading to repeat sales and word-of-mouth recommendations
